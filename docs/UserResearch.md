# User Research

## Observation 1

Observation Statement: Many people are enrolled in extracurricular activities which may cause them to forget about important assignments and deadlines.

Supporting Data: In our survey, about 63% of respondents said that they were enrolled in some type of extracurricular activity.

Team Action (if any): Possibly create a feature to show how said activities line up with other deadlines to better show a user's schedule.

## Observation 2

Observation Statement: Many people procrastinate completing their tasks.

Supporting Data: Majority of survey participants selected that they complete their tasks on the due date or a few days before.

Team Action (if any): Incentivise early completion of tasks.
